# SEO Strategy Playbook & Organic Acquisition Bible

This document establishes the strategic search engine optimization (SEO) roadmap for **ReviewManagement** to drive high-intent organic trial signups and demo requests.

---

## 1. SEO Mission & Core Objectives

* **Primary Goal**: Systematically capture local business operators, agency directors, and franchise managers searching for reputation tooling.
* **Business Goals**:
  * Reduce Paid Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) by building a compounding organic lead engine.
  * Establish domain authority as the premier voice in AI-powered reputation automation.
  * Increase free trial registrations and product demo bookings.
* **Core KPIs**:
  * Organic Trial Signups (Primary Conversion Metric)
  * Average ranking positions of Tier 1 & Tier 2 keywords
  * Consolidated organic traffic volume & click-through rates (CTR)
  * Quality referring domain (backlink) growth count monthly

## 5. Local SEO Roadmap

To capture geographical search queries (e.g., "reputation software Austin"), we will execute the following phased roadmap:

* **Phase 1: Google Business Profile Optimization**:
  * Maintain an active GBP listing for ReviewManagement, accumulating reviews and publishing weekly product announcements.
* **Phase 2: State-Specific Pages**:
  * Deploy routes `/local/[state]` (e.g., `/local/texas`, `/local/california`) highlighting local case studies and regional marketing agency partners.
* **Phase 3: City-Specific Landing Pages**:
  * Deploy sub-routes `/local/[state]/[city]` (e.g., `/local/texas/austin`, `/local/california/los-angeles`) optimized for queries like `review management service austin tx`.

## 6. Structured Schema Architecture

We inject JSON-LD schemas in layout headers to earn rich search result features (snippets, star ratings, and FAQ accordions):

* **SoftwareApplication Schema**: Used on the homepage to denote pricing tiers, application category, and product reviews.
* **Organization Schema**: Used globally to establish corporate details, contact numbers, and social profiles.
* **FAQ Schema**: Used on FAQ pages and the homepage to earn organic search accordion space.
* **Article Schema**: Used on blog pages to declare author details, date published, and content images.
* **Breadcrumb Schema**: Used on nested paths to declare directory hierarchies for search crawling maps.

## 8. Reporting & Analytics Setup

* **Google Analytics 4 (GA4)**: Measure user flow from organic arrivals to `/register` goal completions.
* **Ahrefs / Semrush**: Audit referring domains, monitor competitor backlinks, and track target keyword position changes weekly.
