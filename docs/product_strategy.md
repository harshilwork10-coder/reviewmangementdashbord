# ReviewManagement - Business Strategy & Product Positioning

## Product Vision
ReviewManagement is a SaaS platform that helps businesses collect, monitor, respond to, and improve online reviews across multiple platforms.

* **Mission**: Help businesses turn customer feedback into revenue, trust, and growth.
* **Long-Term Goal**: Become the reputation management platform for SMBs, agencies, and multi-location businesses.

---

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams
1. **Monthly Subscriptions**: Recurring monthly fees across Starter, Growth, and Agency tiers.
2. **Annual Subscriptions**: Discounted yearly plans to improve retention and upfront cash flow.
3. **Agency Plans**: Custom plans tailored for multi-client operations.
4. **Enterprise Plans**: High-value plans for national and regional brands with custom requirements.
5. **Setup & Migration Services**: Professional service fee for importing history and settings from legacy systems (e.g., BirdEye, Podium).
6. **White Label Offering**: Ability for agencies to brand the reporting dashboards as their own.

### Target MRR Goals
* **Initial Milestone**: $10,000+ MRR
* **Growth Milestone**: $50,000+ MRR

## Target Customers & Personas

### Target Segments
* **Primary**: Local Businesses, Restaurants, Medical Practices, Retail Stores
* **Secondary**: Marketing Agencies, Franchise Operators, Multi-Location Businesses
* **Enterprise**: Regional Brands, National Brands

### Customer Personas

#### Persona 1: Small Business Owner
* **Description**: Typically owns a single local establishment.
* **Pain Points**:
  * Low overall review volume compared to competitors.
  * Negative reviews harming local search visibility and client trust.
  * No standard response process or time to deal with complaints.

#### Persona 2: Agency Owner
* **Description**: Manages marketing, SEO, and reputation for dozens of client accounts.
* **Pain Points**:
  * Logging into multiple accounts to track and reply to reviews for different clients.
  * Difficulty providing clean, unified, white-labeled reporting to clients.

#### Persona 3: Multi-Location Manager
* **Description**: Oversees brand image and operational standards across multiple franchise locations or regions.
* **Pain Points**:
  * Inconsistent customer service/reputation management quality across branches.
  * Limited aggregate visibility to understand which locations are performing poorly or well.

## Success Metrics & KPIs

### Business KPIs
* **MRR / ARR**: Tracking recurring revenue progress.
* **Churn Rate**: Measuring customer retention.
* **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)**: Quantifying the average revenue value per customer lifecycle.

### Product KPIs
* **Reviews Generated**: The total volume of reviews collected via the platform.
* **Review Requests Sent**: The quantity of SMS/email feedback invitations sent.
* **AI Replies Generated**: Volume of AI replies generated and approved.
* **Active Locations**: Total locations configured and managed on the platform.
